Harmoniums of Mercury

Slow computer (5k harmoniums) Normal computer (10k harmoniums) Fast computer (40k harmoniums)

How to use

  • Type stuff! Press keys to visualize individual characters
  • Press <enter> word <enter> to visualize whole word
  • Click/wait/spacebar for a random thought

Did you know?

  • Harmoniums were created by Kurt Vonnegut in his book The Sirens of Titan
  • You need a WebGL-enabled web browser to view Harmoniums
  • This is just another useless creation by Ondřej Žára
