Harmoniums of Mercury

Slow computer (200 harmoniums) Normal computer (500 harmoniums) Fast computer (1000 harmoniums)

How to use

  • Press keys to visualize individual characters
  • Press <enter> word <enter> to visualize whole word
  • Click/touch/wait/spacebar for a random thought

Did you know?

  • Harmoniums were created by Kurt Vonnegut in his book The Sirens of Titan
  • Harmoniums do not use images and are not drawn into a canvas
  • This is just another useless creation by Ondřej Žára
